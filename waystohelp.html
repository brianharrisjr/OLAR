There are so many ways you can help a dog or cat.

Host a Pet Drive

Because we are not publicly funded and our fundraising events only take us so far, we are often in short supply of things that our pets really need while in foster care.

The following step-by-step instructions will tell you everything you need to know about hosting a successful pet supply drive at your place of business, your school, your church, or with your extended family members and social groups. Make sure you have appropriate permissions and support from officials within your organization.

Pick a date, plan your promotions, and set a goal! You are ready to get started.
