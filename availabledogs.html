One Love Pets are up-to-date on shots, spayed/neutered, and micro-chipped. They are heart worm and fecal tested, and while in our foster care, we provide heart worm prevention, good nutrition, and flea and tick prevention. Our adoption fees may be tax deductible donations. Consult your tax professional. We are a 501 (c)(3) tax exempt organization.

These donations help us cover the medical expenses and day-to-day needs of all the dogs in our rescue. We do rescue sick dogs at times because these pets need us the most. Our fees are standard for rescues, and help us do the important work of saving homeless pets. One Love is run completely by dedicated volunteers donating their time and opening their homes and hearts to shelter animals.

Interested in adopting? Fill out our application.

One Love Animal Rescue, INC.

The Mission of One Love Animal Rescue is to rescue homeless animals, nurture them through foster care, and place them in permanent loving homes.
